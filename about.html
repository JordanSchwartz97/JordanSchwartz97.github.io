About me

My name is Jordan Schwartz. I was in the US Marines for 4 years. I served with 2nd Battallion 4th marines and 2nd battalion 3rd marines.
While I was in the marine corps I was a mortarman and worked inside the Fire Direction Center(FDC). I deployed overseas twice and got out
of the military in August of 2020. After getting out of the military I wanted to do something more in line with my interests and decided
on going to school for full stack software engineering at Devcodecamp online coding bootcamp.
